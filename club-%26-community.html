pickleball and our community

At Kensington Pickleball Club, we want pickleball to be accessible to as many people as possible throughout our community.  Our club works along with local schools which gives people in the area access to services and spaces that would not usually be available.


We also provide pickleball coaching for the students of schools we work with and ensure this fits in with the schools curriculum.



After School Clubs

We hold junior pickleball sessions after school for ages 3 - 16 years. This is a great opportunity for children in the community to get involved in a sport outside of school which will keep them active and social.

How We Help Our Partner Schools
